GTA, NGHTMRE And Doctor P Top Wobbleland Lineup

Publish date: 2024-05-06

Recommended Videos

San Jose’s two day EDM festival Wobbleland has revealed its lineup for next month, revealing a stacked roster of top notch performers. With major headliners including GTA, Doctor P, Hucci, and 2015’s breakout producer NGHTMRE, Wobbleland is set to be one rager of a party.

Supporting acts slated to perform at Wobbleland 2015 include Getter, Ghastly, Stooki Sound, Troyboi, Liquid Stranger, Andrew Luce, Badklaat, Dr. Fresch, Wuki, and Coyote Kisses. The lineup sports a hefty number of b2b sets as well, between Cookie Monsta and Funtcase, Blankface and Maze, Grimblee and Hecka, Mark Instinct and Twofold, G Jones and Buku, and Twine and ALgo.

With a massive lineup filled with up and coming talent and festival veterans alike, Wobbleland 2016 is sure to be an awesome addition to your festival season agenda.

Wobbleland will opens its gates February 26 through 27 at the City National Civic in San Jose, California. Tickets are available for purchase here.
