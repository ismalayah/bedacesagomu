Who Are Alex Albons Parents Kankamol Albon And Nigel Albon?

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Alexander Albon Ansusinha is a Thai-British Formula One driver for Williams.

Albon earlier raced in Formula One for Scuderia Toro Rosso and Red Bull Racing and DTM for AF Corse.

Who Are Alex Albon’s Parents Kankamol Albon And Nigel Albon?

Alexander Albon was born on March 23, 1996, in London, United Kingdom to Nigel Albon and Kankamol Albon.

The professional F1 racer, Albon, was born in a family of car racers.

Nigel Albon

Alex’s father Nigel Albon is a former British race car driver. He last drove in the Porsche Carrera Cup Asia for Team Vertu in 2007.

The 65-year-old Nigel born on  February 8, 1957, raced in the Renault Clio Cup, finishing the year 5th on points, in 1993.

In the 1994 British Touring Car Championship (BTCC), Nigel raced a Renault 19 for Harlow Motorsport in the Total Independents Cup ranking 5th with 108 points.

In 2001, he drove one race of the FIA GT Championship for the Gamon Porsche team.

Kankamol Albon

The F 1 driver’s mother, Kankamol Albon was convicted of fraud involving the sale of prestige cars from her Smallbridge Hall home, near Bures in 2012.

Almost 200 items were seized from her amid the investigation.

Kankamol was initially investigated after she alleged she had been blackmailed into selling hundreds of prestige cars from her family-run business, N A Carriage Company, for less than the market value.

However, inquiries revealed this was a smokescreen for criminal acts including a fraudulent investment operation, leading to the loss of £7.5m.

In October 2012, Kankamol was sentenced to six years in prison after pleading guilty to fraud by false misrepresentation, fraudulent trading and theft, plus VAT fraud.

Kankamol Albon, 51, currently lives with Alex together with his younger brother and sisters.
