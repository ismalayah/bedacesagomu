Meet Susan Sarandons children: Eva, John and Miles

Publish date: 2024-07-18

SUSAN Sarandon is popularly known for her roles in The Witches of Eastwick and Feud: Bette and Joan.

The Oscar winner's children have appeared to follow in their famous mother's footsteps.

How many children does Susan Sarandon have?

Susan Sarandon has three children; Eva Amurri, John "Jack" Henry Robbins, and Miles Robins.

Eva is Susan's daughter from her past relationship with Franco Amurri.

Jack and Miles are from the Blue Beetle actress' former marriage to her second ex-husband Tim Robbins.

As she continued to experience mainstream success, her journey in motherhood started in her late 30s.

Read More on Susan Sarandon

Regarding her experiences as a late-in-life mother, Susan shared with Good Housekeeping: "I had my first baby at 39 and my third at 45, and with each child, [people were] like, 'Are you crazy? Don't!'

"I think it was 'Don’t have the baby,' as though it would ruin my career, and I was so old anyway.

"Perfection requires too much energy, so I gave it up a while ago.

"All my kids have a sense of humor. I even have a clown nose for having serious conversations, it stops you from getting emotionally loaded."

Who is Susan's daughter Eva Amurri?

Born on March 15, 1985, Eva Amurri is Susan's eldest child from her previous relationship with Franco Amurri.

Through her father, she has two more paternal siblings; Augusta and Leone.

Similar to her famous mother, Eva is also an actress; she and Susan have appeared on-screen together several times.

Eva has co-starred with her mom in Dead Man Walking, Anywhere But Here, The Banger Sisters, That's My Boy, and Mothers and Daughters.

Outside of acting, she is also a lifestyle influencer and the founder of Happily Eva After.

The actress and influencer also has three children of her own; Marlowe Mae, Major James, and Mateo Antoni.

Who is Susan's son Jack Henry Robbins?

Born on May 15, 1989, Jack Henry Robbins is Susan's second child.

He is one of her two sons with her second ex-husband Tim Robbins.

Just like his parents, Jack works as a filmmaker in Hollywood.

He is credited with many short films and TV projects.

From 2021 to 2022, Jack served as a segment director for Jimmy Kimmel Live!

Who is Susan's son Miles Robbins?

Born on May 4, 1992, Miles Robbins is Susan and Tim's youngest son; he's also an actor.

His first acting role was a small part alongside his mother and older sister in Dead Man Walking.
