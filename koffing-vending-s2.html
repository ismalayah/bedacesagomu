For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Koffing.

Koffing (Japanese: ドガース Dogars) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of Expansion Sheet 2.

Card text

Division
ぶんれつ
 
Whenever an attack damages this Pokémon (even if this Pokémon is Knocked Out), you may search your deck for a Basic Pokémon named Koffing and put it onto your Bench. Shuffle your deck afterwards. This power can't be used if your Bench is full or if this Pokémon is Asleep, Confused, or Paralyzed.
Strange Gas
ふしぎなガス		20
Flip a coin. If heads, all Pokémon Powers of your opponent's Pokémon stop working until the end of your opponent's next turn.
