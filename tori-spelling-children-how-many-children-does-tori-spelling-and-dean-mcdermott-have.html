Tori Spelling Children: How many children does Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott have?

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Victoria Davey Spelling was born on 26 May 1973, in Los Angeles, California. She is American but her parents were from Jewish families.

She has a younger brother named Randy. Spelling is an actress and author. In 1990, her first acting role was as Donna Martin on Beverly Hills, 90210.

She has also appeared in A Friend to Die For, A Carol Christmas, both versions of Mother, May I Sleep with Danger? The Mistle-Tones, Sharknado: It’s About Time, Scary Movie 2, The House of Yes, Trick, Izzie’s Way Home and a number of other movies.

Spelling’s book, Stori Telling was named as the best celebrity autobiography in 2009. It also was at the top of the New York Times Best Seller List.

Spelling got married to her first husband, Charlie Shanian, a writer and author on July 3 2004. They however separated in October the next year and eventually got divorced in 2006. By that time, Spelling had already met actor, Dean McDermott. They got married on May 7, 2006, on a private island in Fiji.

The union produced five children; three sons and two daughters. Their names are Liam Aaron, Finn Davey, Beau Dean, Stella Doreen and Hattie Margaret. Spelling is also the stepmother of Jack Montgomery McDermott, McDermott’s son with his former wife, Mary Jo Eustace.
