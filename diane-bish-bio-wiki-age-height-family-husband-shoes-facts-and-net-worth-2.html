Diane Bish Biography

Diane Bish is an American organist, conductor, composer, as well as executive producer and host of The Joy of Music television series who was born in Wichita, Kansas, United States of America. Her career began on a full scholarship at Asbury College in Kentucky, which had a four-manual Austin pipe organ.

In her junior year, Bish transferred to the University of Oklahoma, after auditioning to become a student of Mildred Andrews at the university.

    After she graduated with a Master’s degree, Diane became the organist at the East Heights Methodist Church in Wichita, Kansas.
